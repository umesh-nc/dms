Our mission in Catholic education

"Together, we ensure that every Catholic school student is formed in the tradition and grows into the person God created them to be."

The Alleluia Ball is the signature fundraising event of the Catholic Community Foundation. Funds raised support teacher professional development, student tuition assistance, and school technology innovation across Northeast Ohio.
