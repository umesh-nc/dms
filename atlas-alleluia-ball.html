Alleluia Ball 2027
October 30, 2027 · Huntington Convention Center, Cleveland OH
Recommendations for 2027 — based on 2025 data
Open Dream Maker and Visionary tiers 6 months early. In 2025, Dream Maker had remaining capacity while all other tiers sold out. Offering early-bird access to top-tier sponsors 6 months in advance gives major donors first-mover recognition and allows the committee to plan table seating earlier.
Increase Good Shepherd from 8 to 12 tables. The $5,000 tier sold out and had significant waitlist demand. At 12 tables, this tier would generate an additional $20,000 in revenue and accommodate more mid-level major gift prospects who are appropriate for this giving level.
Invite the 14 cultivation prospects from 2025 as priority registrants. Mike & Meredith Hudec, the Contis, and the Valentines should all be personally invited to chair or co-chair 2027. Their combined LTG of $414,000 and demonstrated event commitment makes them the strongest chair candidates in the portfolio.
Add a planned giving recognition moment to the programme. Launa Barboza and David Shipman both have legacy society affiliation. A brief on-stage recognition of bequests and planned gifts — without naming amounts — creates a social norm among attendees and has been shown to increase planned giving conversations in the following 12 months.
