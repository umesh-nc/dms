Identity Donor identity checks 6 rules
Donor not found Error
Typed string matches no record. Could be a typo, new donor, or name mismatch.
Input: "Hennessy, R" → No match
Suggest: Hennessy, Robert (#4821) — last gave Apr 2025
Show top 3 fuzzy matches ranked by similarity + last gift date
Ambiguous name — multiple matches Warning
Typed name matches 2 or more records. Common with "Walsh, John" in large diocesan databases.
Input: "Walsh, Thomas" → 4 records:
#1102 Walsh, Thomas F. · #3847 Walsh, Thomas G. · …
Force selection before row can post. Show gift history alongside each match.
Household vs. individual mismatch Warning
Gift attributed to household record when prior gifts went to the individual, or vice versa. Creates split giving history.
Prior: O'Brien, James (#881) · New: O'Brien Household (#880)
Suggest matching the attribution pattern from the prior year.
Deceased donor flag Info
Donor record is marked deceased. Gift may be valid (estate/bequest) or a wrong-record error.
Sullivan, Margaret (deceased Mar 2025) — gift dated Apr 2026
Surface notice; prompt clerk to confirm or redirect to estate record.
Do not solicit flag Warning
Donor record has a DNS/DNC flag. An unsolicited gift from a DNS donor is fine to post, but staff should be aware.
Costello, Barbara — DNS flag set Apr 2024
Display flag visibly. Require acknowledgement before posting.
Lapsed donor re-engagement Info
Donor has not given in 24+ months. Gift is valid but flagged so a gift officer can be notified.
Walsh, Susan — last gift Jun 2023 (22 months ago)
Post normally; auto-create a task for the assigned gift officer.
Amount Gift amount intelligence 7 rules
Statistical outlier vs. giving history — the core rule Warning
The entered amount deviates significantly from this donor's historical giving pattern for the same fund. Atlas computes a personalised threshold per donor using their last 5 gifts, flagging anything beyond a configurable multiplier (default 3×). A donor who always gives $250 suddenly giving $25,000 is almost certainly a transposition error.
Sullivan, Margaret — CMA history: $2,500 / $2,500 / $2,000 / $2,500 / $3,000
New entry: $25,000 → ⚠ 10× typical amount (possible: meant $2,500?)
Show donor's giving history chart inline. Offer one-click correction to the most common prior amount. Require explicit confirmation to proceed.
Transposition error detection Warning
Entered amount is a digit-permutation of a historical amount — classic data entry error.
History: $1,250 · Entered: $2,150 → transposition?
History: $500 · Entered: $5,000 → extra zero?
Suggest the most likely intended amount alongside the entered one.
Negative or zero amount Error
Amount is zero or negative. Zero is a skipped field; negative requires a reversing entry workflow.
Amount: $0.00 → empty field
Amount: -$500 → use Reversal Entry instead
Block posting. For negatives, redirect to the Reversal Entry workflow.
First-ever gift — unusually large Warning
Donor has no prior giving history and the gift is above a configurable threshold (default $1,000).
Mendez, Carlos — no prior gifts · Amount: $10,000
→ New major donor or entry error?
Prompt: confirm this is a new major donor, or wrong record?
Year-over-year upgrade / downgrade Info
Gift is significantly higher (upgrade ≥50%) or lower (downgrade ≥50%) than the same campaign in the prior year.
CMA 2025: $1,000 · CMA 2026: $250 → 75% downgrade
CMA 2025: $500 · CMA 2026: $2,500 → 5× upgrade — confirm?
Upgrade: notify gift officer. Downgrade: auto-log lapse-risk task.
Giving capacity check (WealthEngine) Info
Gift amount exceeds the donor's estimated capacity from WealthEngine screening. Not an error — donors may exceed model predictions — but worth flagging.
Capacity estimate: $5K–$10K/yr · Entered: $50,000
Info badge only. Auto-create officer task.
Round-number / pledge type check Info
Very large round number significantly above history. May need pledge record instead of one-time gift.
History: $750–$1,500 · Entered: $100,000
→ Should this be a pledge, not a cash gift?
Suggest switching gift type to Pledge if amount exceeds 10× prior single gifts.
Duplicates Duplicate & repeat detection 4 rules
Same check number already posted Error
Check/reference number already exists on a posted gift. Strongest duplicate signal — check numbers are unique per account.
Ref #10441 — already posted Mar 15 to the same donor
Hard block. Show the original posted gift for comparison.
Same donor, same amount, recent date Warning
Exact match on donor + amount within a configurable window (default 30 days). Most common duplicate pattern — a cheque batch re-entered or online gift also posted manually.
Sullivan, Margaret — $2,500 CMA · Mar 15 (existing)
New row: Sullivan, Margaret — $2,500 CMA · Apr 3
Show prior gift inline. Require "confirm not a duplicate" before posting.
Same donor appears twice in batch Warning
Donor appears twice in the current batch with the same fund. Likely a duplicate row from Ctrl+D or copy-paste.
Rows 3 and 7 both: Walsh, Susan — CMA — $100
Highlight both rows. Offer one-click delete of the duplicate row.
Online gift already auto-imported Info
A GiveCentral online gift matching this donor, fund, and amount is already imported. Prevents double-counting when a clerk manually enters what was already auto-imported.
Kim, Patricia — $250 CMA — Apr 15 — online ref #OG-88421 already in system
Block with a clear explanation and link to the existing online gift record.

Pledge over-payment Warning
A payment against a pledge would push the cumulative total beyond the original pledge amount.
Pledge #PL-2026-0047: $250,000 · Paid: $200,000
New entry: $100,000 → total would be $300,000
Show pledge balance inline. Require confirmation or split into a new gift for the excess.

New gift: no restriction specified

Dates Date & payment logic 5 rules
Date outside batch date range Warning
A row's gift date falls significantly outside the range of other rows. Could be a mis-typed year or a stale cheque in the wrong batch.
Batch rows: Apr 14–18 2026 · Row 9: Apr 14 2025 (last year)
Flag the outlier row visually. Show the difference clearly.
Future date Warning
Gift date is in the future. Unusual for a cheque or credit card payment; possible for a pledge commitment.
Gift date: May 30, 2026 · Today: Apr 18, 2026
Warn for cash/cheque/credit. Allow for pledges. Require confirmation.
Stale cheque Info
Cheque date is more than 6 months prior to today. Bank may not honour it; finance should be alerted before depositing.
Cheque dated Oct 12, 2025 · Entered Apr 18, 2026 (6 months old)
Info flag. Notify the finance team before bank deposit.
Tax year boundary Info
Gift date is in the prior tax year but being entered now. The donor's receipt must show the gift date, not the entry date.
Gift date: Dec 28, 2025 · Entry date: Jan 14, 2026
→ Receipt year: 2025
Info banner confirming receipt year. Recommend explicit acknowledgement.
DAF gift — non-deductible benefits Error
A DAF gift is being posted to an event fund that includes a personal benefit (ticket, dinner). DAF distributions cannot cover benefits.
Sullivan Foundation (DAF) — $25,000 to Bishop's Gala
→ $1,400 ticket value cannot be DAF-funded
Block. Require split: DAF amount = pledge minus benefit value. Separate personal payment record required for benefit portion.
