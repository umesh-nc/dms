About the event

A day of community, competition, and charity

The Bishop's Golf Classic is one of the most beloved annual events in the Diocese of St. Petersburg, bringing together Catholic faithful, community leaders, and supporters of Catholic education for a day of fellowship on the greens.

Now in its 12th year, the tournament raises critical funds for the Angel Scholarship Fund, providing tuition assistance for families who might otherwise be unable to afford a Catholic school education for their children.

Last year's event raised over $128,000 — funding more than 40 full-year scholarships. All skill levels are welcome. This is a day of community as much as competition.
