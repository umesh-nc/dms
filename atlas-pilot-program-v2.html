Terms & eligibility
Who qualifies, and what's required
The pilot is a mutual commitment — GiveCentral brings reduced fees and white-glove service; the diocese brings an openness to share their experience.
Eligibility criteria
Existing GiveCentral client — strongly preferred. Non-GC clients considered for strong strategic fit.
Currently using Raiser's Edge as primary donor CRM
Active development office with at least one dedicated data staff member
Donor database between 50K and 500K records
Able to begin migration within 90 days of signing
Leadership sponsor (development director or CFO) committed to the project
Not eligible: clients mid-contract with RE with more than 12 months remaining, unless RE waives early termination

GiveCentral's commitment
What we provide to pilot clients
Six concrete deliverables — not vague promises — that GiveCentral guarantees to every founding partner client.
Dedicated migration lead
A named GiveCentral project manager owns the migration end-to-end. Single point of contact from kickoff through go-live.
50% migration discount
Fixed-price migration at half the standard rate. Price is locked at signing — no time-and-materials overruns, ever.
Priority scheduling
Pilot clients get first access to implementation slots. Migration starts within 30 days of kickoff — no queue.
Extended onboarding
Double the standard training hours at no extra cost. Custom sessions for each role — gift officers, data clerks, finance, development director.
Roadmap input
A seat at the table for quarterly product roadmap reviews. Feature requests from pilots are prioritised ahead of the general release queue.
Beta feature access
First access to new Atlas features before general availability — with the option to provide feedback before public release.
Implementation timeline
Signing to case study — 6 months
A structured 6-phase journey from pilot agreement through published case study and reference activation.
Pilot program timeline

Onboarding & migration phase
1

2
Weeks 3–4
Discovery & data audit
GiveCentral audits the RE export. Fund structure, GL codes, donor IDs, and giving history mapped to Atlas schema. Migration plan confirmed and fixed price locked.
3
Weeks 5–12
Migration & configuration
Full data migration runs in staging. Diocese staff validate against RE. Parish hierarchy, fund structure, and user roles configured. Parallel run period begins.
Go-live & case study phase
